Spawns an isolated sub-agent worker in the background for massive tasks.

- Use for parallel exploration, multi-step research, or isolated sub-tasks that would pollute your context.
- Available agent types: explore, general, build, plan.
- The tool returns a session ID immediately. The subagent runs asynchronously.
- When the subagent finishes, it will inject a message into your chat history.
- Prefer this over doing everything in the main conversation when tasks are massive.
